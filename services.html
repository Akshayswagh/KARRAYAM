Dheya’s Offering for Scholarship Providers:

Dheya empowers scholarship providers with tailored solutions to identify and support deserving youth from Tier 2 & 3 towns and rural areas. We ensure a streamlined approach by creating accurate digital profiles of candidates, supported by insights into their family background, academic achievements, and aspirations. .

Key Offerings:

  • Targeted Data Collection - Analyse data of deserving youth with right academic performance through school records, test scores, and co-curricular achievements.
  • Comprehensive Candidate Profiling Assess behavioural and psychometric insights to understand students' potential and career aspirations.
  • Real-Time Monitoring - Track scholarship utilization and student progress through live dashboards..

Benefits for Scholarship Providers:

  • Impactful Allocation: Focus resources on students with genuine need and potential.
  • Transparency: Ensure a fair, data-backed selection process..
  • Empower Communities: Nurture local talent.
  • Custom Solutions: Design scholarships to align with specific goals. .
